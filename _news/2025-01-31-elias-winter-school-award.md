Received the **Best Project Award** for _Procedure Understanding with Vision-Language Models_ with [Zehao Wang](https://homes.esat.kuleuven.be/~zwang/) and [Alessandro Sottovia](https://www.unibz.it/en/faculties/engineering/phd-advanced-systems-engineering/phd-students/phd/52920-alessandro-sottovia) at the [ELIAS–ELLIS–VISMAC Winter School 2025](https://elias-ai.eu/event/ellis-vismac/) in Brunico, Italy (Jan 27–31, 2025). The project evaluated pre-trained VLMs in inference-only mode for predicting procedural action sequences and dependencies in egocentric video.
