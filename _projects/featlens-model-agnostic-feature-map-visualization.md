Model-agnostic feature-map visualization for visual foundation models. FeatLens renders dense **feature maps** from **any** vision backbone and **any layer** as a model × layer grid, with **PCA**, cosine-similarity seeds, **k-means**, foreground masks, saliency, cross-image correspondence, and attention rollout.
